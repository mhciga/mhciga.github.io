2017 My Mini Field Project
I launched a project called My Mini Field Project, which was a six-month plan encouraging students to discuss, interview and report on important social issues. I led twenty students to write on diverse topics including gender issues, minorities, and land and social justice. The reports were published on a well-known online media platform in Taiwan and were shared over 500 times on Facebook. In the end, the twenty students and I collaborated to hold a fantastic exhibition of reports, attracting more than 5,000 people to visit.
